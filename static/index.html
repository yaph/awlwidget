How to find the URL of your Amazon Wish List

  • Log onto your Amazon account.
  • Click on the Your Lists button at the top right of the page and select Wish List.
  • In the left sidebar, mark your list as public if necessary and click on link of your list in the Your Public Wish Lists.
  • Copy the complete URL (e.g. http://www.amazon.com/gp/registry/wishlist/YOURLISTID/ref=cm_wl_rlist_go) of your wish list from the address bar of your browser and paste it in the My Wishlist URL field in the widget configuration.
